Pound Cake recipe

BEST EVER pound cake recipe you would stick forever

Ingredients

  • As much powderly stuff as you'd think is needed for wonderful poundcake
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Some little tasty stuff as you like

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients nicely and put 'em into a cake tin.
  2. Bake in the oven until cooked through lovely.
  3. Bon Appetit!
