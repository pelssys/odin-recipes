Gyoza recipe

Pan-fried Japanese dumpling filled with a wonderful mixture of minced beef and pork and veges.
Perfect recipe for making skins from scrach if you like it.

Ingredients

Servings:24

For skins

  • 200g All purpose flour, sieved
  • 100ml Hot boilied water

For filling

  • 150g Minced beef and pork
  • 150g Chinese cabegge, chopped and well drained
  • 30g Chives, chopped
  • 1 piece of garlic, chopped
  • 1 piece of ginger, chopped

Seasonings

  • 30g Chiken stock powder
  • 15g Soy source
  • 10g Sesami oil
  • 15g Cooking sake

Steps

For skins

  1. Put flour in a bowl and add boilied water little by little as string flour with chopsticks.
  2. Knead the dough until it comes together.
  3. Place the dough in the fridge for 1 hour.
  4. Tear off as much dough as needed to roll out with a stick to form 10cm diameter skins.

For gyoza

  1. Knead minced beef and pork with all seadonings.
  2. Add chopped Chinese cabagge and chopped chives to the meat and lightly mix them.
  3. Wrap filling with skins.
  4. Pan fry gyoza with some sesami oil.
